My Journey

My fascination with artificial intelligence began during my early years of engineering studies at ITM Vocational University. The idea that machines could learn, understand, and make decisions just like humans captivated me and set me on a path of continuous learning and innovation.

Throughout my academic journey, I immersed myself in machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. The breakthrough moment came when I successfully developed an AI-based Healthcare Assistant that earned recognition at the national level.

Today, as an ML Engineer in an R&D environment, I work on cutting-edge projects that involve developing Python-based preprocessing pipelines, integrating complex data systems, and creating solutions that have real-world impact.
